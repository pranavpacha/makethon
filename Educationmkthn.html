Vidya Niketan School (Grade 1-10)

I embarked on my academic journey at Vidya Niketan School, Hebbal, Bangalore, where I pursued the ICSE syllabus from 1st to 10th grade. The years spent in this esteemed institution were transformative, fostering a solid foundation in various subjects. It gives me great pride to share that upon reaching the culmination of my high school journey, I successfully graduated from the 10th grade with a commendable 90% percentage in the ICSE board examinations. These formative years not only instilled in me a passion for learning but also equipped me with the skills and knowledge essential for my future endeavors.


Mallya Aditi International School

Continuing my academic voyage, I transitioned to Mallya Aditi International School, Bangalore, for my 11th and 12th grades, pursuing the ISC syllabus. These two pivotal years were akin to a pre-university phase, serving as a stepping stone for my impending college and university life. Mallya Aditi International School provided an enriching environment that not only broadened my academic horizons but also honed my critical thinking and analytical skills. The comprehensive curriculum and supportive community at the school played a crucial role in preparing me for the challenges and opportunities that awaited in my higher education journey.


RV University

Embarking on a new chapter of my academic journey, I am currently enrolled at RV University, Bangalore, pursuing a B.Tech (Hons) in Computer Science Engineering. As a first-year student in the first semester, the experience has been nothing short of captivating. The thrill of navigating the diverse world of college life, coupled with the excitement of delving into the intricacies of coding and computer science, has truly been an exhilarating adventure. I am relishing the challenges and opportunities that come with this new academic pursuit. Envisioning the next four years at RV University, I am poised to graduate in 2027 with a wealth of knowledge and experiences that will shape my future in the field of Computer Science Engineering.
