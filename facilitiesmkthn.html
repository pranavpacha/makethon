Facilities

Training Grounds

We have state-of-the-art training grounds equipped with the latest equipment and maintained to the highest standards.


Click the links below for our two training/match ground locations:-


Training Ground 1 location : Padukone - Dravid Centre for Sports Excellence

Training Ground 2 location : Matchday Bel Road

Gym and Fitness Center

Our gym and fitness center features modern workout machines and equipment to help our players stay fit and healthy.

Physiotherapy and Medical Facilities

We provide on-site physiotherapy and medical facilities to ensure the well-being of our players.

Locker Rooms and Showers

Our locker rooms and showers are designed for comfort and convenience, providing players with a space to relax and refresh after training sessions.

Video Analysis Room

We utilize video analysis to review and improve our players' performance. Our video analysis room is equipped with the latest technology for this purpose.
